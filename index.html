Our Mission

"The truth is not revealed all at once, but petal by petal."

The crown chakra, often referred to as a thousand-petaled lotus, is said to be the most subtle chakra in the system, relating to pure consciousness, and it is from this chakra that all the other chakras emanate. Our RYT-certified teachers guide you through physical classes in the here-and-now with gentle guidance to remind you of your higher self as well as the unity and interconnectivity of all things. At Lotus Yoga we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive space where you can open your mind and heart, and with your breath dive deeper into the truth of yourself and your life, one class at a time.
